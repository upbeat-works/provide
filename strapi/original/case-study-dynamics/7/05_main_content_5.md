These graphs show how Cooling degree hours (expressed in °C*h/yr) and Lost working hours per year for intense activities (expressed in h/yr) will play out over time on average over the urban area of Nassau, according to the scenarios SSP1-1.9 and SSP5-8.5. The lines indicate the median estimates for these scenarios, while the coloured bars show the respective confidence intervals in 2090. They illustrate model uncertainty arising from imperfect knowledge of the amount of global warming caused by greenhouse gas emissions and of the local response to global warming. The urban area over which the results are averaged can be seen on the maps below.
