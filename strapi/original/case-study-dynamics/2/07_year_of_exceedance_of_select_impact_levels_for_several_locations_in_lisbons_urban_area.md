For the Lisbon's urban area as well as 2 locations indicated on the map, the table shows when in the 21st century the probability for the number of days per year with very high heat stress to exceed different levels of impact (indicated on top of each column) would exceed 5% (first row) or 50% (second row) for three scenarios of greenhouse gas emissions (2020 climate policies, Delayed action and Shifting pathway). Urban hot spot: centre of Almada; urban medium spot: north-east of Lisbon municipality. The urban area referred to here is visible on the maps in the previous sections.
