This scenario assumes a moderate decrease in countries’ emissions as stated in their 2020 Nationally Determined Contributions (NDCs). Then, as a thought experiment, global warming peaks at 2.7°C shortly after 2100 and goes back to 1.5°C by 2300 (best estimate) to study if climate impacts would be reversed as a result.
