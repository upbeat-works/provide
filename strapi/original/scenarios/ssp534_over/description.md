This pathway was designed to explore the sensitivity of the Earth System to unrealistically high emission levels before 2040, followed by unrealistically rapid decreases after 2040 through deployment of negative emissions technologies. Global warming peaks at 2.3°C in 2060 then goes back to 1.9°C by 2100 (best estimate).
