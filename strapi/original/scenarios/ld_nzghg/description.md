Strong decarbonisation efforts are undertaken, with a focus on energy demand reductions by implementing energy efficient lifestyles and technologies. Once net zero greenhouse gas emissions is reached, both emissions and aerosols are kept constant. Global warming peaks at 1.5°C in 2040 and goes back to 1.2°C by 2300 (best estimate).
