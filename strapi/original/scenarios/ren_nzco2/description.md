Strong decarbonisation efforts are undertaken, with a focus on renewable energy deployment. Global warming peaks at 1.6°C in 2040, then declines to 1.5°C until net zero CO2 emissions are reached. Both emissions and aerosols are then kept constant, leading to warming of 1.6°C by 2300 (best estimate).
