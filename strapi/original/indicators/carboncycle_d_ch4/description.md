Annual mean methane (CH4) concentration anomaly in the atmosphere due to peatland emissions. The net impact of northern peatland on the global CH4 concentration is calculate as the difference between experiment where OSCAR-peat module was turned on (peatland carbon cycle is simulated) and the experiment where when OSCAR-peat module was turned off (peatland carbon cycle is not simulated). Changes in this indicator are expressed in parts per billion (ppb).
