Methane (CH4) emissions from northern peatlands. In northern peatlands, CH4 emissions arise primarily from the decomposition of organic matter in peatlands, and can be influenced by factors such as temperature variations, organic matter availability and water table levels. The higher the peatland CH4 emission anomaly, the more CH4 emissions there are from peatlands. Changes in this indicator are expressed in teragrams of carbon per year (TgC yr-1).
