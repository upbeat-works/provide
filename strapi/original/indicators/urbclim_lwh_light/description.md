Annual number of working hours lost because of heat stress, for light activities (such as drawing, typing or sewing). The limits for safe working conditions under heat stress conditions are defined by ISO 7243 and Hooyberghs et al. (Climatic Change, 2017). Light activities are assumed to be affected as soon as wet-bulb globe temperature (WBGT) exceeds 31°C and to become impossible when WBGT reaches 33°C. WBGT is a measure of heat stress which takes into account temperature, humidity, wind speed, sun angle, shading and cloud cover.
